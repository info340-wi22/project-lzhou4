App Description

Users of the application include, Foodies (People interested in making, exploring, and trying different and/or new food), people interested in finding community based on food interest, and restaurants owners interested in expanding their menus to gain customer traction. Information users will be viewing is food genres, rankings, restaurants and dishes. Users can be able to filter out and pick which genre of food they prefer if they want. All food genres will be available for users to explore. Rankings will show restaurants and dishes. In addition, users can be able to make their own ranking list and share their list to others.

Users can create their own food ranking in different categories. For example, one might go to the Japanese restaurant section and upload their list of favorite Japanese restaurants. Users will be able to search by food genre and look at rankings by others. For example, under the Japanese restaurant section, they are able to see what others put for their favorite. This app will help users reflect upon their top most food interests to be able to make decisions based on ranking system of food. This will be an intuitive organized method for users to navigate their food interests which can be useful to engage with other users based on a similar interests of foods. Thus overall, brining together a community through the connection of food!
