Top 1:

Korean Tofu House

Korean Tofu House has the best side dishes!!! And the dishes all taste so good! It's my go-to place when I feel like eating Korean food.

Bring me there!

Top 2:

The BoB

The BoB have very good side dishes as well, but the wait time there is a bit longer. I LOVE their spicy pork bone soup and bibimbap!

Bring me there!

Top 3:

Chi Mac

The fried chicken at Chi Mac is THE BEST!!! I absolutely love the snow flavor, other flavors are good too! Their fried rice and cheese corn are also very delicious. Also love their radish that goes with the chicken!

Bring me there!

Top 4:

Kong Tofu House

Kong Tofu House is very affordable and tastes okay, I want to try all of their menu.

Bring me there!
