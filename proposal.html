Project Proposal

Problem Description

When hanging out with friends, there comes a moment when all members of the group's stomach grumbles. We all have had the problem of picking a common ground for choosing a place to eat out. Chick-fil-A? Burger King? Olive Garden? A study found that on average, we spend about 40 minutes thinking about food. This can be very time consuming and mentally laborious process just to find a what or where to eat. All these times spent figuring out food interests should have been used to revert back to productivity or actually exploring these food interests

This kind of problem exists because there is a gap in sharing information. Your friend might not know your dining habit because people usually keep their preferences in their hearts and compromise the choice of other people. That's why we need a platform that record our food preferences and be able to share with friends to better understand each other's eating habit and help decide on the restaurants to explore together.

One existing web app that addresses food preferences problems is TopDish. TopDish allows users to record their own ratings for restaurants and dishes. It accumulates the average rating for each dish and sets a score for that dish. This web app also has a feature for Deals. With this feature, it can help users compromise their food choices. However, TopDish is only available for people living in the United Kingdom and does not allow users to rank their preferences.

App Description

Users of the application include, Foodies (People interested in making, exploring, and trying different and/or new food), people interested in finding community based on food interest, and restaurants owners interested in expanding their menus to gain customer traction. Information users will be viewing is food genres, rankings, restaurants and dishes. Users can be able to filter out and pick which genre of food they prefer if they want. All food genres will be available for users to explore. Rankings will show restaurants and dishes. In addition, users can be able to make their own ranking list and share their list to others.

Users can create their own food ranking in different categories. For example, one might go to the Japanese restaurant section and upload their list of favorite Japanese restaurants. Users will be able to search by food genre and look at rankings by others. For example, under the Japanese restaurant section, they are able to see what others put for their favorite. This app will help users reflect upon their top most food interests to be able to make decisions based on ranking system of food. This will be an intuitive organized method for users to navigate their food interests which can be useful to engage with other users based on a similar interests of foods. Thus overall, brining together a community through the connection of food!
